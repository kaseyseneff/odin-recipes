One Pot Spinach Mushroom Pasta

Description

This one pot spinach-mushroom pasta comes together in a flash. Pairs well with chicken but is great with pork chops, steak, and fish too

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 yellow onion, sliced
  • 8 ounces white mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon crushed red chile flakes
  • 1 tspn kosher salt
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 8 ounces rotini pasta
  • 1 (5 ounce) bag baby spinach
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1 tspn lemon juice

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium high heat. Add onion and mushrooms. Cook for 7 minutes. Add garlic and crushed chile flakes. Cook for 1 minute.
  2. Stir in salt, broth, and pasta. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat, cover, and cook at a simmer for 10 minutes. stirring halfway through.
  3. Turn off heat. Add spinach, cover, and let sit for 2 minutes. Stir in Parmesan and lemon juice. Serve immediately.
